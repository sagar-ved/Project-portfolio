About me

Hi, I'm Sagar Ved, a passionate software engineer specializing in building secure and scalable backend systems. With a strong foundation in computer science and a love for cybersecurity, I thrive on solving complex problems and bringing innovative ideas to life.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from IIIT Vadodara. Over the past few years, I've honed my skills in Java Spring Boot, distributed systems, and cybersecurity, working on projects ranging from payment gateways to enterprise-level web applications.

My technical expertise includes Java, Spring Boot, RESTful APIs, and database management with PostgreSQL. I'm also proficient in DevOps practices, including CI/CD, containerization with Docker, and cloud deployment on AWS.

In addition to my professional work, I'm actively involved in open-source projects. I'm currently developing 'Asterisk', an open-source distributed payment gateway network designed to provide secure and scalable transaction solutions.

When I'm not coding, I love exploring the latest trends in cybersecurity, contributing to open-source communities, and diving into the world of Ubuntu. I'm also a fan of reading about new technologies and experimenting with them in my free time.

I'm always eager to learn new things and take on challenging projects. If you're looking for a dedicated and skilled software engineer to join your team, feel free to reach out!

Let's connect! You can reach me at sagarvedverma@gmail.com or follow me on LinkedIn at sagarvedverma.
